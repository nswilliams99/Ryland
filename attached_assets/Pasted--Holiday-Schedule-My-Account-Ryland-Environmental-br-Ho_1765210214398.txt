Cart Placement
Place carts 3-5 feet from roadway and 3-5 feet from any obstructions such as poles, mailboxes, cars, other carts, etc.

Handles should face the house, arrows on the lid face towards the curbside.


Please bag all trash to help prevent litter

Carts must be out by 6:00 a.m.
(Suggested night before)

Each customer can schedule 2 bulky items per week. This is included in your standard residential service. Bulky items are defined as items too large to fit inside the trash cart. Examples include furniture and appliances. Construction debris or extra bags of garbage not covered.
