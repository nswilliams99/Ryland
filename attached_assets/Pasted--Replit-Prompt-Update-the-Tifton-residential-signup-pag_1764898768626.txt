**H2:** Holiday Schedule

Pickup is delayed by ONE day after these holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Thanksgiving
- Christmas

Example: If the holiday is Monday, Monday pickup moves to Tuesday, Tuesday to Wednesday, and so on through Saturday.
